# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

GlowGuide is committed to being an inclusive, respectful, and beginner-friendly project. Contributors and users should feel welcome regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Use respectful and inclusive language.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Avoid judgmental comments about bodies, skin, hygiene, or personal routines.
- Respect that personal care needs differ across people.
- Prioritize safety and clarity.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Shaming users for personal care choices.
- Submitting unsafe, misleading, or promotional content.
- Publishing medical claims without review.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, reject contributions, or block participation if behavior harms the safety and inclusiveness of the project.
